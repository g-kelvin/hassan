I am Hussein Hassan, a transformational Leadership Coach based in Nairobi Kenya. The firm has been providing unique bespoke in-house and public leadership development and management training courses for over ten years. We tailor all our training to suit your organisation’s needs, whether that’s communication skills, influencing, assertiveness, or presentation skills courses. All participants on our management and leadership training courses leave with something specific to them that will make a real difference in their lives.

Passionate about transformational training. We don’t just teach groundbreaking methods, we ensure you experience a permanent shift in your Public Speaking skills. Our methods are fun, creative and build on your existing and hidden strengths. Our training uniquely combines highly interactive small group work with coaching for your individual needs. As professional speakers we will model what works for you. You benefit from our experience without the associated challenges or time costs, transforming your ability quickly in a safe environment.

    Hussein Hassan founded Success Blueprint Hussein Hassan in 2008 in response to a demand for high quality communication training courses in London. Over several years Hussein developed the Serlin Method™, which infuses performance, psychology and coaching. Hussein does Public Speakings, One on One Coaching, Group Coaching and Masterminding. Hussein Hassan consists of a small team of business professionals with backgrounds and experience in both learning and development and operational leadership. We have delivered training, development and coaching solutions across a wide range of business sectors in the Kenya and internationally

    MASTERMINDING

    Mastermind group is a peer-to-peer mentoring to help our members. The intention is that we help each other solve problems and develope ourselves through input and advice from each other.
    The core value of the Mastermind Group is the synergy of energy, motivation, and commitment as well as the willingness to learn and grow together. The requirement is commitment, confidentiality, willingness to both give and receive advice and ideas, and support each other with total honesty, respect and compassion.
