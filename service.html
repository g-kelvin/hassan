    One on One Coaching

    What is executive coaching?
    Executive coaching or “1:1 coaching” is tailored leadership development for senior executives, other leaders and high potentials.
    We work with you as the leader 1:1 on your goals and challenges, your particular strengths and any areas for development. Together we create a strong and trusting partnership that allows you to gain greater self-awareness, tap into your potential, broaden your leadership skills and change behaviors.

    This personalized approach to leadership development acts as a catalyst to enhance your performance as the leader and the teams or organization that you lead.



    TEAM DEVELOPMEMT

    How would better teamwork affect productivity in your organization?
    What difference would higher levels of team effectiveness make in achieving organizational goals?
    Team development workshops and retreats help to build cohesion among employees. Our training solutions provide open, non-threatening environments to encourage interaction and communication among individuals that carries over into their working relationships and teams.
    Together we explore how each member contributes to the team through individual efforts. We teach the skills needed for team members to capitalize on each member’s role and work synergistically as a team. Teamwork starts with great leadership and this is central to the team’s success. In addition to the team leadership training provided in the High-Performance Team development Workshop, our focus will be on the four principles of team development
    1. Strong structure (LEADERSHIP)
    2. Compelling direction, (VISION)
    3. Supportive context—(OPPORTUNITIES FOR GROWTH)
    4. Shared mindset (VALUES)
    We reinforce team development principles with group activities to illustrate the importance of working as a team for maximum productivity and group satisfaction.

    PUBLIC SPEAKING

  • Public speaking course consists of 7 days (one day a week) of public speaking training and coaching on advanced presentation skills. It empowers leaders to deliver speeches and presentations effectively. You will learn to create and deliver powerful, persuasive and memorable presentations.

  • You will also master interpersonal communication across all formats including challenging areas such as handling hard questions and moderating panels.

  • This course consists primarily of practical workshops and simulations. You will have many opportunities to practice new speaking techniques in a positive and encouraging environment.

  • You will receive individual performance coaching with specific learning points.

    • This course covers the following modules:
  • Advanced storytelling

  • Advanced body language for public speaking

  • Preparing for hard questions

  • Group formats and panel discussions

    • CONFLICT RESOLUTION

    Conflict Management training seminar entitled Managing Conflicts & Difficult Situations has been specifically designed to help leaders and managers to control and reduce negative conflict, build high-trust organizational cultures and deal proactively with difficult situations using highly effective techniques. The programme incorporates the latest research regarding Emotional Intelligence (EI), neuro-science, psychology, personality research and cutting-edge communication and influencing techniques.
    Conflict resolution is a daily occurrence at work that can either propel or disrupt the momentum for a leader, a team or the entire organization. Leaders with conflict management skills have the ability to address and resolve the contradictory interests of two or more parties in a high-emotion, low-trust environment. When conflict is managed effectively, it can bring new levels of understanding, empathy and trust between parties. In this Seminar/workshop, you will learn communication methods to increase understanding, which helps to break down conflict and allows for problem solving.
